Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A complete backend-powered platform with 8 modules working seamlessly together.

👤

User Management

Role-based access for Admins, Organizers and Participants. Account verification, profile management, and secure authentication.

📅

Event Scheduling

Assign venues, set dates, check availability automatically, and prevent scheduling conflicts across all your events.

✅

Approval Workflow

Admin-controlled approval pipeline. Events move from Draft to Pending to Approved with email notifications at every step.

🎟️

Smart Registration

Capacity-aware registration with automatic waitlisting. Track attendance, send confirmations, and export participant lists.

💳

Payment Processing

Support for multiple payment methods. Track payment status, generate receipts, and process refunds when events are cancelled.

🎓

Auto Certificates

Certificates are generated automatically after event completion. Participants can download and verify using their certificate number.

How it works

From idea to event in 4 steps

1
Create your event

Organizers fill in event details, set venue, date, capacity and submit for review.

2
Admin approves

Admin reviews the event, adds remarks if needed, and approves or rejects with one click.

3
Participants register

Approved events go live. Participants browse, register, and pay. Waitlisting is automatic.

4
Certificates issued

After the event, attendees receive feedback prompts and downloadable certificates automatically.

Built for everyone

One platform, three roles

Every user gets a tailored experience designed for exactly what they need to do.

  • Verify organizer accounts

  • View payment reports

Participant
